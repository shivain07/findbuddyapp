FindyBuddy - Child Safety & CSAE Standards

At FindyBuddy, we are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy toward any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE).

Our Commitment

  • We strictly prohibit any content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • We monitor user-generated content and take swift action against violations.
  • Accounts involved in such activities are permanently banned.
  • We cooperate fully with law enforcement authorities when required.

Platform Safety Measures

  • Users can report inappropriate content directly within the app.
  • We review and remove harmful content promptly.
  • We continuously improve moderation tools to enhance user safety.
