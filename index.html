Welcome to FindBuddy

FindBuddy is a simple yet powerful social web application designed to help you connect with friends and discover meaningful relationships. Whether you're looking to expand your social circle or find like-minded individuals, FindBuddy makes it easy and enjoyable.

Key Features

👥 Connect

Build meaningful connections with people who share your interests and values.

💬 Communicate

Real-time messaging to stay in touch with your friends and buddies.

🎯 Discover

Find friends based on interests, location, and preferences.

🔐 Secure

Your privacy and security are our top priorities.

About This Project

FindBuddy is built with modern web technologies to provide a seamless user experience. Our tech stack includes TypeScript, JavaScript, and CSS for a robust and responsive application.
